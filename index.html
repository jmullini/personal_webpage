About Me

Hi! I'm James and I am the Senior Software Engineering Manager within the Advanced Structures BU of Collins Aerospace. I primarily work on building AI-enabled manufacturing solutions in the cloud, but we work on a wide array of products and technologies as well. Large scale data processing is at the core of what we do and at the cornerstone of optimizing our global operational network.

I am a graduate of the Engineering Science program at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, specialized in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. My undergraduate education was in Management Science at the University of California, San Diego. I am currently nominated to be a Technical Fellow at Collins Aerospace.
